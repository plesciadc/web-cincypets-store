About Project

My projects differ in a variety of aspects from works of other photographers. On my website you will not find any ordinary photos and shots that can be characterized as dull and boring. In this project I tried to underline the emotional meaning of love and long-lasting relationship. This project features professional models portraying an initial idea of love and passion in these photos.
